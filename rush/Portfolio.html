"Participated in the Life Skills, Volunteer Spirit, Moral and Environmental Development Camp at Toei Ngam Beach, Marine Corps Camp, Sattahip Naval Base."

Participated in the Life Skills, Volunteer Spirit, Moral and Environmental Development Camp at Toei Ngam Beach, Marine Corps Camp, Sattahip Naval Base. The project aimed to develop participants’ life skills, foster volunteerism and moral values, and promote environmental conservation. Activities included teamwork exercises, volunteer activities, environmental clean-up and preservation tasks, as well as workshops on ethical decision-making. Through these activities, participants gained practical skills, strengthened their social responsibility, and developed a deeper understanding of the importance of environmental conservation.
